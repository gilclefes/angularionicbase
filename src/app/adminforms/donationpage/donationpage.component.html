Donate Your Clothes

Yabo will collect your clothing donations and deliver them to a local non-profit supporting our community.

To donate, just place your gently worn items into a paper or plastic bag labeled “Clothing Donation.” Your Valet will collect it during your next Rinse pickup or delivery and deliver it to our local charity partner.

To learn which organizations your donation will support, reach out to care@yaboclean.com.
